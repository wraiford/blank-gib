About IbGib Protocol...

The ibgib protocol is the culmination of a lifetime of work.

Originally inspired by SuperMemo, automatons and information-centric ontology like memes, ibgib has evolved as a unique DLT approach:

  • unique content addressing mechanism
  • unique first-class citizens of timelines, i.e., spacetime
  • unique distributed computation identity (planned)
